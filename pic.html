These are my favorite rock bands:

Slipknot is an American heavy metal band formed in Des Moines, Iowa, in 1995 by percussionist Shawn Crahan, drummer Joey Jordison and bassist Paul Gray. Slipknot is well known for its attention-grabbing image, aggressive style of music, and energetic and chaotic live shows.
SOAD (also known as SoaD or simply System) is an Armenian-American heavy metal band formed in Glendale, California, in 1994. he band has used a wide range of instruments, such as electric mandolins, baritone electric guitars, acoustic guitars, ouds, sitars, and twelve string guitars.
AC\DC is an Australian rock band formed in Sydney in 1973 by Scottish-born brothers Malcolm and Angus Young. Their music has been variously described as hard rock, blues rock, and heavy metal, but the band themselves call it simply "rock and roll".
Rammstein is a German Neue Deutsche Härte band formed in Berlin in 1994. Rammstein's sound has primarily been described as industrial metal, hard rock, and gothic metal, while also being described as nu, alternative, symphonic, progressive and "techno-metal".
Eisbrecher is a German Neue Deutsche Härte band. The band's lyrics and slogans often include terms of ice and sailing, such as "Ahoi" ("Ahoy") and "Es wird kalt" ("It's getting cold"); vocalist Alexander Wesselsky often wears naval and military clothing in performances.
